Mimic Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Mimic ("the App") values user privacy and handles information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Camera permission status (for front camera preview features).
  • Video data selected by the user from device storage (for playback features).

2. Purpose of Use

The collected information is used only to provide core app features, including video playback and front camera preview.

3. External Transmission and Analytics

At this time, the App does not include ad SDKs, analytics SDKs, or crash analytics tools, and does not transmit personal data to external servers.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not provide collected information to third parties except as required by applicable law.

5. Retention and Deletion

The App may temporarily store data on the device only as necessary for feature operation. Deleting the App removes app data from the user environment.

6. Use by Minors

The App has no age restriction. Minors should use the App under guardian supervision when appropriate.
